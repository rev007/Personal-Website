Football Squares the Game

An App Story

Football Squares the Game was designed for the iPad and allows users to play football squares interactively. Instead of getting stuck with lousy squares after the initial drawing, players answer trivia questions and, if they’re correct, get rewarded with a free move to a new square!

Let’s go through the user experience (ux) design process and explain the overall goals for improving the product and delivering a better user experience.

All right, so we've all played football squares and we know what they are, right? If not, you can get a quick primer here... otherwise it will be difficult to follow along.

Premise

The conceptual model is a 1950s electric football game where player tokens vibrate randomly along a smooth metallic gridiron. In our case, the tokens vibrate if a player decides to move after answering a trivia question correctly.
