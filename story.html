Football Squares the Game – An App Story

This application was designed for the iPad and allows users to play football squares interactively. Instead of getting stuck with lousy squares after the initial drawing, players answer trivia questions and, if they’re correct, get rewarded with a free move to a new square!

Let’s go through the user experience (ux) design process with the overall goal of improving the product and delivering a better user experience than if designed without.

Premise

The conceptual model is a 1950s electric football game where player tokens vibrate randomly along a smooth metallic gridiron. In our case, the tokens vibrate if a player decides to move after answering a trivia question correctly.
